Our Story

ABOUT THE
CAMPAIGN

The Uganda AIDS Marathon 2026 is a powerful movement uniting runners, communities, and advocates in the fight against HIV/AIDS. Organized by Village Journeys Africa in partnership with the Uganda AIDS Commission, this annual event harnesses the energy of sport to raise awareness, reduce stigma, and fund life-saving programs across Uganda.

The Torch of Hope Campaign carries the flame of awareness from city to city — 13 regional activations across Uganda — building momentum for the Grand Marathon on 29th November 2026 in Kampala, the heart of the nation.

🔥

Torch of Hope

13 regional activations carrying the flame of hope from Arua to Mbarara, uniting communities against HIV/AIDS ahead of the Grand Marathon.

🖼

Story Gallery

Grand Marathon

29th November 2026 — thousands of runners converging in Kampala for the ultimate celebration of hope, life, and solidarity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does my UGX 30,000 kit include?
A race bib and a branded water bottle. Additional kit details will be announced as the event approaches.
Can I register if I'm outside Kampala?
Yes. The Torch of Hope activations run in 13 regions across Uganda before the Grand Marathon — check the Road Map for a city near you.
How do I pay for my kit?
Mobile money (Airtel Money & MTN MoMo) merchant codes will be published here once live. In the meantime, message us on WhatsApp to reserve your spot.
